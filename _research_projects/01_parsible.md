# About Parsible

Parsible is a web app to guide users through reading primary scientific literature. Each section of a scientific paper is broken down into chunks, allowing users to work through it step by step, learning how to read critically and gain understanding. I have also included some background information on scientific publishing in general, a stats refresher, links to additional resources, practice games, and 2 papers to work through alone. It is currently only available in English, but I have added a toggle to render it in dyslexia-friendly font. The audience is advanced secondary to undergrad, but is likely also useful for PhD students and even non-experts out of higher education.
